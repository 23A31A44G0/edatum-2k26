About EDATUM 2k26 | STRIDES

EDATUM 2k26 is an annual event organized by the Department to showcase technical talent and cultural expression. Since its inception, it has grown to host national and international participants across data science, innovation sprints, workshops, and cultural programs.

Scale & Reputation

We host multi-track competitions, attract industry mentors, and feature guest speakers. Our past editions have awarded top-performing teams and provided internships and mentorship opportunities.

Audience

Students (UG/PG), researchers, and early-stage startups are encouraged to participate. Registrations are open to national and international attendees.

Vision

We aim for technical and cultural inclusion — providing equitable opportunities for learning, showcasing, and collaboration. Events include mentorship panels, hands-on labs, and cultural showcases.
